About A1 Auto Detailing

A1 started with a love for cars and a drive to make a name through quality work.

Austin Arnold built A1 Auto Detailing from the ground up on his own, starting local and earning trust one vehicle at a time.

In the beginning, he detailed cars out of his parents' driveway while building his skills, growing his client base, and learning what it takes to run a business the right way.

Today, that same mindset still leads A1: honest service, disciplined process, and results that help every customer feel proud of what they drive.
